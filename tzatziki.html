Tzatziki Chickpea Salad

Think of this Chickpea Salad as a better-for-you potato salad. Except â€“ it can do so much more! Chickpeas mixed with an extra chunky tzatziki dressing, use this to make protein-laden salad bowls, super-food sandwiches, serve as a side or scoop with crackers.

Ingredient Amount
Cucumbers 2
Greek Yogurt 310g
Lemon Juice 1 tbsp
Olive Oil 2 1/2 tbsp
Fresh Dill 0.25 cup
Garlic Clove 1
Kosher Salt 1/2 tsp
Chickpeas 800g
Celery 0.75 cup
Capers 2 tbsp

Step 1

Put the Tzatziki ingredients in a big bowl and mix. Add the chickpeas, celery and capers, mix again.

Step 2

You can eat it straight away but it's better if you can set aside for at least 1 hour to let the flavours meld (especially salt from capers & the garlic). It's even better the next day. Give it a taste and add a pinch of extra salt if needed (capers saltiness can differ).

Step 3

Serve drizzled with extra olive oil and extra dill. Dive in!
