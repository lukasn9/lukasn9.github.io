Green Papaya Salad

Listed in CNN Travel’s Top 50 Foods of the World, Green Papaya Salad is everything you know and love about South-East Asian salads. This Thai salad – called Som Tum – is a riot of fresh colours and crunchy textures, brought to life with a signature sour-sweet-savoury dressing spiked with a whack of fresh chilli. Now THIS is what I call a salad!!!

Ingredient Amount
Garlic 2 tbsp
Bird Eye Chillies 6
Dried Shrimp 6 tbsp
Palm Sugar 150g
Lime Juice 125 ml
Fish Sauce 125 ml
Roasted Peanuts 1 cup
Snake Beans 20
Grape Tomato 3 cups
Green Papaya 500g
Thai Basil Leaves 0.5 cups

Step 1

Put the Tzatziki ingredients in a big bowl and mix. Add the chickpeas, celery and capers, mix again.

Step 2

You can eat it straight away but it's better if you can set aside for at least 1 hour to let the flavours meld (especially salt from capers & the garlic). It's even better the next day. Give it a taste and add a pinch of extra salt if needed (capers saltiness can differ).

Step 3

Serve drizzled with extra olive oil and extra dill. Dive in!
