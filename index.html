Carbonara

This is a real spaghetti carbonara recipe made the traditional Italian way, without a single drop of cream. It relies solely on egg and cheese to make a luscious, creamy carbonara sauce. Food fit for a king (or queen!) that proves simple can be magnificent.

Ingredient Amount
Guanciale 175g
Eggs 2
Egg Yolks 2
Parmigiano Reggiano 100g
Black Pepper 0.25 tsp
Spaghetti 400g
Kosher Salt 1 tbsp
Pasta Water 125 ml
Gralic Clove 1

Green Papaya Salad

Listed in CNN Travel’s Top 50 Foods of the World, Green Papaya Salad is everything you know and love about South-East Asian salads. This Thai salad – called Som Tum – is a riot of fresh colours and crunchy textures, brought to life with a signature sour-sweet-savoury dressing spiked with a whack of fresh chilli. Now THIS is what I call a salad!!!

Ingredient Amount
Garlic 2 tbsp
Bird Eye Chillies 6
Dried Shrimp 6 tbsp
Palm Sugar 150g
Lime Juice 125 ml
Fish Sauce 125 ml
Roasted Peanuts 1 cup
Snake Beans 20
Grape Tomato 3 cups
Green Papaya 500g
Thai Basil Leaves 0.5 cups

Maple Glazed Ham

Maple Glazed Ham – this is the ham glaze to use when you want to add a special touch to your festive baked ham, but still keep it easy! The most incredible sticky glaze with the subtle fragrance of maple and hint of holiday spices, this is THE Christmas Ham recipe I make to gift and take to gatherings year after year.

Ingredient Amount
Leg Ham 5kg
Cloves 30
Oranges 2
Water 1 cup
Maple Syrup 3/4 cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Dijon Mustard 3 tbsp
Ground Cinnamon 3/4 tsp
All Spice 1/2 tsp
